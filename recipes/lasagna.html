Steps

  1. Make the meat sausce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixtuer
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving

Layering Order

The layering order for lasagna typically follows this sequence to ensure a balanced distribution of flavors and textures:

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzerella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining parmesan
